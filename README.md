## 🌟 Project Overview
An end-to-end exploratory data analysis of over **90,000+ order records** from the Olist Store. This project transforms raw complex data into actionable strategic insights and management protocols to drive profitability and operational efficiency.

## 📁 Repository Contents
- **Dashboards of Olist Market.pbit**: Power BI Template (Lightweight structural file).
- **Olist Market Report.pdf**: Full strategic management report.
- **Data Set Source(Essential for Analysis !).txt**: Project documentation and setup guide.

---

## 🎯 Strategic Analysis Coverage
The project provides a 360-degree view of the business through four main pillars:

1. **Geographic & Strategic Analysis**: Identifying sales hotspots and market concentration risks.
2. **Logistics & Supply Chain**: Evaluating delivery efficiency and SLA compliance.
3. **Service Quality & Customer Satisfaction**: Correlation analysis between delivery speed and customer ratings.
4. **Financial Performance & Order Status**: Tracking revenue health and success rates.

---

## 💡 Key Strategic Insights & Features
* **Geographic Risk Management**: Identified high revenue dependency on São Paulo and Rio de Janeiro; proposed diversification strategies to mitigate sustainability risks.
* **Supply Chain Optimization**: Linked delivery delays to specific geographical conditions to develop targeted logistics solutions.
* **Customer Behavior Insights**: Analyzed the impact of "Bank Approval Time" and "Shipping Costs" on customer reviews to improve retention.
* **Category Health Check**: Identified declining product categories and provided actionable recovery solutions.
* **Cancellation Forensics**: Analyzed factors influencing order cancellations to optimize the checkout-to-delivery pipeline.

---

## 🛠️ Methodology & Data Re-engineering
### 1. Advanced ETL & Cleaning
- Removed logical errors and incomplete records (missing dates/delivery info).
- **Time-Frame Filtering**: Retained data from Jan 2017 to Aug 2018 to ensure a realistic and reliable performance baseline.
- **Feature Engineering**: Created custom columns for `Final Status`, `SLA Compliance`, and `Delivery Days`.

### 2. Dynamic Dashboards
Developed **four interactive dashboards**, each telling a specific narrative:
- **General Performance**: High-level KPIs and geographic distribution.
- **Logistics Deep-Dive**: Shipping efficiency and delay analysis.
- **Customer Voice**: Sentiment and rating patterns.
- **Financial Status**: Order lifecycle and approval latency.

### 3. Executive Management Report
Compiled all findings into a structured **Management Protocol** PDF, providing decision-makers with a clear roadmap to increase efficiency and avoid potential risks.

## 🚀 How to Run the Project
Since the dataset is large, I have provided a **Power BI Template (.pbit)**:

1. **Download Data**: Get the Olist dataset from [Kaggle Source](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/olistbr/brazilian-ecommerce).
2. **Open Template**: Open the `Report.pbit` file using Power BI Desktop.
3. **Set Path**: When prompted, enter your local folder path in the **MainFolder** parameter.
4. **Load**: Click 'Load' and the report will populate with the latest data.
